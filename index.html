Affiliates Mailing List PLS EMAIL US

 Tue 15 April 2014 by Michael Kelly

Now that we've released a brand new Firefox Affiliates site, we could use some feedback and suggestions. We also want to be better at being open about Affiliates development, and an easy and useful step is to actively use a public mailing list for discussion among the team members.

Thus, I revived the affiliates@lists.mozilla.org as a place for the team to discuss our work as well as a place for people to leave feedback, ask questions, or get involved with making Affiliates a more effective service.

You should totally check the site out and send feedback to the mailing list. Like right now.
